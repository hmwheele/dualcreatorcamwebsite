Privacy Policy

Collects nothing. By design.

Last updated · April 2026

Dual Creator Cam (“the app”) is a dual-camera iPhone utility published by Harrison Wheeler. This document describes what the app does and does not collect.

What the app collects

Nothing. The app does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on remote servers. The app does not include analytics, advertising SDKs, third-party trackers, or crash reporting services that send data off the device.

How recordings are stored

Video files recorded by the app are temporarily stored in the app’s local sandbox while a recording is in progress. When a recording finishes, the file is moved to your Photos library (with your permission). The temporary copy in the app’s sandbox is then no longer needed and may be cleaned up by the system.

Recordings are never uploaded anywhere. They live on your device unless you share them through iOS share sheets, AirDrop, Files, or another app.

Permissions

PermissionReason
CameraRequired to capture video and photos.
MicrophoneRequired to record audio with your videos. Optional — if denied, recordings will be silent.
Photo Library (add-only)Required to save finished recordings to your Photos library.

You can change any of these in Settings → Dual Creator Cam at any time.

Children’s privacy

The app is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect any data about children or anyone else.

Changes to this policy

If this policy is ever updated, the new version will be published at this URL and the “Last updated” date above will be revised. Material changes will be highlighted in the app’s release notes.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to hello@dualcreatorcam.com. For help using the app, see the support page.
